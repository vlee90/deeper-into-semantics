Welcome to Gere-a-Deli

The Gere-a-Deli has been feeding Anacortes since 1981. Located in historic downtown Anacortes, the deli has remained a family owned and operated business.

It's no secret to the locals why the Gere-a-Deli is the place to go for lunch, or why they've voted us best deli soup, salad, business lunch, quick lunch, chowder, sandwich and catering.

There's something here for everyone! Meatloaf, bumble berry cobbler, soups, salads, sandwiches, desserts, pastries, rolls and a selection of old fashioned bottled soda you'll not find from anywhere else!
